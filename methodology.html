Methodology

T he methodology for this research project focuses on developing an AI-enhanced HVAC system that optimizes energy efficiency and user comfort, specifically for Sri Lanka's tropical climate. The development of AC systems originated from the increasing demand to control the thermal comfort of indoor environments. Moreover, these systems were well known for their high energy consumption, which led to a pressing need for more intelligent and efficient solutions. The proposed methodology is organized into several stages to achieve energy efficiency while maintaining the optimal comfort level by optimizing several important parameters within air conditioning systems. During the proposed work, first of all by analyzing previous trends and all, decide how many numbers of Acs should be turned on in order to achieve energy efficiency while maintaining the optimal comfort level. Once after the number of Acs defines, which Acs should be turned on factor also takes into consideration. After deciding number of Acs and which Acs, now it’s time to focus on the important factors like airflow, temperature and humidity. Adjust the airflow based on the occupant presence, controlling room temperature based on several environmental factors and at the same time control the fan speed to adjust the room temperature for its ideal level and also control the relative humidity inside the space automatically without manual intervention, makes the proposed work unique and a sustainable solution.
